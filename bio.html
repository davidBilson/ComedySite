Claudia Cogan is a comic as affable as she is matter-of-fact. And her approach is working: she recently hopped a ride on Night Train with Wyatt Cenac on STARZ, made it to semi-finalist on Season 7 of Last Comic Standing and won the first ever Time Out New York Joke of the Year. She was featured in New York Magazine's Ten People Funny People Find Funny list. She has been seen at Bridgetown Comedy Festival, San Francisco SketchFest, the Eugene Mirman Comedy Festival and clubs all over. Her work has been featured on Lifetime TV, Rooftop Comedy and Youtube's Official Comedy Channel..
